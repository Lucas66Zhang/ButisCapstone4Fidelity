The text is a Letter of Acceptance, Waiver and Consent between FINRA and Transamerica Capital, Inc. regarding violations of FINRA registration rules from December 2019 to February 2021. Transamerica Capital failed to register, or timely register, over 400 call center personnel who were associated with the firm and engaged in securities business requiring registration. To resolve the matter, Transamerica Capital agreed to be censured and pay a $500,000 fine. By February 2021, the firm had approximately 175 registered individuals handling securities transactions at the call centers and established policies to prohibit unregistered personnel from addressing such requests.
