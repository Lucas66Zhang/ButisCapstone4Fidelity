Between June 2016 and July 2019, Atul Makharia and seven other registered representatives from CFI's Lexington, South Carolina branch office recommended VRSPs to 94 retail customers for whom such investments were unsuitable. As a result, CFI was censured, received a penalty of $750,000, disgorgement of $4,876 and also paid $623 in interest (grand total of $755,509).
