CFI, Mantei, and Makharia agreed to settle charges that they recommended unsuitable
structured products to senior investors without conducting the required suitability
reviews. CFI was censured, Mantei was suspended from acting in a supervisory capacity for
6 months, and Makharia was suspended from association with any broker, dealer, investment
adviser, municipal securities dealer, municipal advisor, transfer agent, or nationally recognized
statistical rating organization for 6 months. CFI agreed to pay disgorgement of $4,876 plus
prejudgment interest of $623 and a civil money penalty of $750,000. Mantei agreed to pay
disgorgement of $92,650 plus prejudgment interest of $11,842 and a civil money penalty of
$206,000. Makharia agreed to pay a civil money penalty in the amount of $35,000.
