The text is a FINRA Letter of Acceptance, Waiver and Consent between FINRA and SpeedRoute LLC regarding violations from September 2016 to February 2019. SpeedRoute improperly accepted 128 market orders to purchase shares of new issues prior to the commencement of secondary market trading, in violation of FINRA Rule 5131(d)(4). SpeedRoute also failed to have reasonable supervisory systems and procedures relating to market orders for new issues. To settle the charges, SpeedRoute consented to a censure and $45,000 fine.
