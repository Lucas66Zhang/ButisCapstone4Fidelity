The text is a NYSE Arca Letter of Acceptance, Waiver and Consent between NYSE Arca and Maxim Group LLC regarding violations of SEC Rule 15c3-5 and NYSE Arca Rules from May 2019 to present. Maxim failed to establish reasonable risk management controls related to credit limits and erroneous order controls for market access. Maxim also failed to have supervisory systems and procedures reasonably designed to achieve compliance with Rule 15c3-5. To settle the charges, Maxim agreed to a censure and $40,000 fine.
