The text is a NYSE American Letter of Acceptance, Waiver and Consent between NYSE American and Citigroup Global Markets regarding a violation of NYSE American Rule 16 (Business Conduct) on March 8, 2022. A Citi trader improperly handled a customer options order by confirming a price with a floor broker but then seeking to cancel the executed trade at that price, resulting in worse execution for the customer. To settle the charge, Citi agreed to a censure and $45,000 fine. Citi also provided restitution to the harmed customer.
