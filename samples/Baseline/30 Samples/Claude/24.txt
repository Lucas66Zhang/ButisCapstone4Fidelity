The text is a NYSE Arca Letter of Acceptance, Waiver and Consent between NYSE Arca and Oppenheimer & Co. regarding violations on April 19, 2022. Oppenheimer failed to adhere to principles of good business practice when handling a customer options order by adjusting the price of an already executed trade without obtaining customer consent. Oppenheimer also failed to have reasonable supervisory systems and procedures concerning options order handling. To settle the charges, Oppenheimer consented to a censure and $90,000 fine. The firm offered restitution of $25,400 to the harmed customer.
