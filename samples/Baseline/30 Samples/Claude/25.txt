The text is a FINRA Letter of Acceptance, Waiver and Consent between FINRA and Baker Tilly Capital regarding violations in 2019. Baker Tilly served as placement agent for two private placements offered on an "all-or-none" basis. The issuer amended the terms of the offerings in material ways, including reducing the minimum offering amount, without terminating the offerings and returning funds as required by SEC Rule 10b-9. For the violations, Baker Tilly consented to a censure, $90,000 fine, and a willful findings resulting in statutory disqualification.
