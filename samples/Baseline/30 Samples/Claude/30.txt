The text is a NYSE American Letter of Acceptance, Waiver and Consent between NYSE American and J.P. Morgan Securities regarding violations on August 2, 2019 and October 11, 2022. J.P. Morgan traders improperly engaged in anticipatory hedging of customer options orders by trading the underlying securities before the terms of the options orders were disclosed to the market. J.P. Morgan also failed to have supervisory systems reasonably designed to prevent such anticipatory hedging. To settle the charges, J.P. Morgan consented to a censure and $65,000 fine.
