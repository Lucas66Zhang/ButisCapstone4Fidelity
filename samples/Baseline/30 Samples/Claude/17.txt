The text is a Letter of Acceptance, Waiver and Consent (AWC) between FINRA and SageTrader, LLC regarding violations of Regulation SHO and FINRA rules from July 2017 to August 2022. SageTrader mismarked over 9 million short sale orders as long and failed to locate shares prior to effecting over 490,000 short sales. The firm also failed to establish and enforce reasonable supervisory procedures concerning order marking and locate requirements for short sales. To resolve the matter, SageTrader agreed to be censured and pay a $175,000 fine.
