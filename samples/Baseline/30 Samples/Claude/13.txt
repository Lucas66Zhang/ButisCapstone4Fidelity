The Cboe C2 Exchange found that on various dates between January 2021 and August 2022, Susquehanna Securities exceeded applicable position limits in certain equity options contracts that were not delta neutral. This violated Cboe C2 Exchange Rules 8.30 and 5.60 incorporating Cboe Exchange Rule 8.30 related to position limits. Susquehanna had prior similar violations in 2021 and 2018-2019. As a result, Susquehanna was censured and fined $8,000 by the Cboe C2 Exchange. Susquehanna consented to the findings and sanctions without admitting or denying them.
